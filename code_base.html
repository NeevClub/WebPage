About Our Club

NEEV Club at Rustamji Institute of Technology is a comprehensive career preparation hub dedicated to helping students achieve their professional goals. While our primary focus is on GATE preparation, we also provide guidance for campus placements, banking sector opportunities, and PSU examinations. Our holistic approach ensures students are well-prepared for diverse career paths in engineering and beyond.
